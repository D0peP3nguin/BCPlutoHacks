Flash Flood Warning

What is a Flash Flood?

A flash flood is a sudden and intense flood that occurs within six hours of heavy rainfall, dam or levee failure, or the sudden release of water.

Precautions to Take

  • Move to higher ground immediately.
  • Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters.
  • Stay informed by monitoring weather updates.
  • Prepare an emergency kit with essentials.

During a Flash Flood

Stay indoors and avoid contact with floodwater. If you are outdoors, seek higher ground immediately and avoid low-lying areas.
